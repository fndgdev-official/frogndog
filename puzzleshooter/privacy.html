2. 수집하는 정보와 이용 목적

운영자는 이용자의 이름·이메일·전화번호 등 개인을 직접 식별할 수 있는 정보를 직접 수집하지 않습니다. 다만 본 앱에 포함된 제3자 서비스(광고·결제·게임 엔진)가 서비스 제공을 위해 아래와 같은 정보를 처리할 수 있습니다.

가. 광고 (Google AdMob)

  • 처리 정보: 광고 식별자(Advertising ID), 기기 정보(모델·OS·언어 등), 앱 상호작용 데이터

  • 처리 주체: Google AdMob / Google Play 서비스

나. 인앱 결제 (Google Play 결제 · Unity IAP)

  • 처리 정보: 구매 내역(예: Supporter Pack 등 상품 구매 기록)
  • 이용 목적: 결제 처리 및 구매 상품 지급
  • 결제는 Google이 처리하며, 운영자는 신용카드 번호 등 결제 수단 정보를 직접 수집하거나 저장하지 않습니다.

다. 게임 저장 데이터

  • 처리 정보: 게임 진행도, 환경 설정 등
  • 저장 위치: 이용자 기기 내부(로컬)에만 저장됩니다.
  • 본 앱은 별도의 계정/로그인 기능이 없으며, 게임 저장 데이터를 운영자 서버로 전송하지 않습니다.

2. Information We Process and Why

The Operator does not directly collect personally identifying information such as your name, email, or phone number. However, third-party services bundled in the App (advertising, payments, game engine) may process the following information to provide their services.

a. Advertising (Google AdMob)

  • Data processed: Advertising ID, device information (model, OS, language, etc.), app interaction data
  • Purpose: To serve personalized or non-personalized ads, and to provide in-game rewards through rewarded ads (hearts, continue, point buff)
  • Processed by: Google AdMob / Google Play services

b. In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing · Unity IAP)

  • Data processed: Purchase history (e.g., Supporter Pack and other items)
  • Purpose: To process payments and deliver purchased items
  • Payments are processed by Google. The Operator does not directly collect or store payment details such as credit card numbers.

c. Game Save Data

  • Data processed: Game progress, settings, etc.
  • Storage: Stored only locally on your device.
  • The App has no account or login feature and does not transmit game save data to the Operator’s servers.

3. Third-Party Services (SDKs) and Policy Links

The App uses the third-party services below. For details on how each service processes data, please refer to its policy.

  • Google AdMob · Google Play services — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Unity (game engine) — unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Game data stored locally on your device is removed when you uninstall the App.
  • You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device settings. (Android: Settings → Google → Ads)

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is a general-audience service and is not primarily directed at children under 13. The Operator does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
